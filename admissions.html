Enrollment Roadmap

Admission Process

Step 1: Enquiry & Registration

Submit basic parent details via our online application form below or visit the school campus helpdesk to obtain a physical prospectus kit.

Step 2: Document Submission

Furnish essential verification certificates including official birth certificate, Aadhaar card copies, photographs, and previous class transcripts.

Step 3: Parent & Student Interaction

A warm, welcoming, and friendly interaction cycle to understand the student's unique learning styles, habits, and developmental potential.

Step 4: Admission Confirmation

Receive our official selection notification, settle the first enrollment fee installment, secure the roll card, and obtain the text syllabus papers.

Verification Checklist

Required Documents

Birth Certificate

Official birth certificate duplicate authenticated by government municipal authorities.

Previous Academic Records

Academic performance scorecards or official progress sheets from the previous class.

Passport-size Photos

3 recent color photograph files of the student and 2 of parent/guardian.

Transfer Certificate

Original Transfer Certificate (T.C.) countersigned by state authorities (if applicable).

Aadhaar Card Copy

Government identification card copy of student as well as parent.

Parent Contact details

Valid residential proof, phone contacts, and active email address credentials.
