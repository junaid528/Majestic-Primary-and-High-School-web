Administrative Directive

A Message from the President

"Education is the most powerful tool to shape minds, build character, and prepare children for a successful future."

Dear Parents, Students, and Well-Wishers,

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to Majestic Primary and High School, an school committed to academic excellence and holistic development.

Our institution holds deep beliefs that true performance is never merely born from mechanical standard practices. It is nurtured through values, deep mental clarity, critical self-governance, and active service responsibility in each pupil.

We strive to provide a meticulously balanced and highly modern learning environment where students grow intellectually, morally, socially, and emotionally with confidence.

At Majestic School, we aim to construct the next generation of social pillars who stand ready to sculpt creative and empathetic solutions for society with absolute knowledge, integrity, and compassion.

Together with our seasoned educators, highly cooperative parents, and inspired student body, we march securely into a bright tomorrow.

Thank you for your trust and collaboration.

Warm regards,

Arif A. Mehkri

President, Majestic Primary and High School

Academic Direction

A Message from the Secretary

"True education inspires learning, strengthens values, and empowers children to serve society."

Dear Students, Parents, and Faculty,

Education forms the absolute foundation of a highly successful, orderly, and responsible civic lifestyle. At Majestic Primary and High School, we remain deeply dedicated to maintaining premium instruction fused with strong character teaching.

We believe every single child carries a unique spectrum of cognitive talent and potential. It stands as our highest institutional responsibility to discover and nurture these traits through targeted mentoring, healthy structures, and ample playground development.

Our core primary directive hinges upon structured classroom progress, balanced moral discipline, creative language mastery, and high self-confidence.

Utilizing low teacher-student layouts, specialized study guides, and diagnostic worksheets, we ensure that no child faces academic hesitation or lags behind target speeds.

Let us continue working hand in hand to champion premium growth outcomes for our brilliant students and our proud institution.

Thank you.

Sincerely,

Prof. Nabijan

Secretary, Majestic Primary and High School

Governance Framework

Our Leadership Values

Visionary Leadership

Charting a progressive path for modern educational frameworks while preserving Indian values and traditional roots.

Academic Excellence

Empowering students with critical thinking, rigorous conceptual clarity, and academic perseverance to top KSEEB boards.

Moral Values

Cultivating honesty, humility, respectful conduct, and empathy to guide students into positive contributors of society.

Student Development

Fostering physical fitness, emotional maturity, logic and speech clarity through active extra-curricular formats.

Discipline & Integrity

Instilling clear code of conduct, absolute uniformity, regular attendance habits, and self-governance traits.

Community Growth

Aligning teachers, parents, and administrative supports to build a warm, helpful, and safe learning environment.

The Vision

To be Mysore’s beacon of excellence in primary and secondary education, cultivating innovative thinkers, highly disciplined learners, and morally upright citizens who succeed globally while preserving local civic values and compassion.

The Mission

To deliver an affordable yet premium academic curriculum that fuses scholastic rigor, modern ICT learning, tactile scientific curiosity, and moral teaching—empowering parents, teachers, and children to shape minds for a better tomorrow.
