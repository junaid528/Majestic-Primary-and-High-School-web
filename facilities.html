Our Facilities

Home / Facilities

Modern Learning Infrastructure

Science Labs

Fully equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology to foster hands-on learning and scientific inquiry.

Computer Lab

State-of-the-art computing facilities with high-speed internet, latest software, and coding programs for digital literacy.

Library

A comprehensive collection of books, journals, digital resources, and quiet study spaces to encourage reading and research.

Sports Grounds

Spacious playgrounds and courts for various outdoor and indoor sports, promoting physical health and teamwork.

Smart Classrooms

Interactive classrooms with multimedia tools and projectors to enhance learning experiences.

Art & Music Room

Dedicated spaces for creative expression through art, music, and cultural activities.

Transportation

Safe and reliable school bus services covering various routes for student convenience.

Cafeteria

Nutritious and hygienic meals prepared under supervision to ensure student health.

Additional Amenities

Medical Room

On-campus medical facilities with qualified staff for emergency care and health check-ups.

Security

24/7 security surveillance and trained personnel to ensure a safe learning environment.

Parent Portal

Online portal for parents to track attendance, grades, and communicate with teachers.

Extracurricular Activities

Wide range of clubs and activities including debate, drama, and environmental clubs.
