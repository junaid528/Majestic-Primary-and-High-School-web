Parent Guidelines

The Academic & Moral Trust

We believe that a child’s comprehensive evolution is a dynamic partnership between home and school. By establishing synchronization, mutual trust, and supportive communication, we enable our students to achieve maximum heights of intelligence and values.

Core Partnership

Parent Responsibility Overview

Daily Diary Check

Examine your child's handbook daily to track notes and write replies diligently.

Attendance Support

Support regular attendance (85%+ target) and absolute daily punctuality habits.

Homework Monitoring

Actively review homework assignments and encourage regular self-study sessions.

PTM Participation

Participate meaningfully in regular PTM sessions to coordinate learning curves.

Uniform & Grooming Check

Ensure your child leaves for school in clean, well-ironed uniforms everyday.

Positive Home Support

Provide a clean, focused, quiet environment at home to cultivate learning mindset.

Operational Directives

Essential Shared Guidelines

Attendance Support

Ensure your child attends school regularly and on time. Punctuality builds a mindset of stability and discipline. Keep leave requests to actual medical exigencies only.

Task & Progress Tracking

Check the School Diary daily. Review assigned homework, academic reports, and teacher notices. Signing the diary daily is critical to maintaining high accountability.

Behavior & Attire Discipline

Foster cleanliness and proper uniform discipline at home. Ensure uniforms are neat and clean, well-ironed, with shoes cleaned. Empower children to respect and obey teachers.

Academic PTM Engagement

Attend all Parent-Teacher Meetings (PTM). Dialogues must remain constructive, positive, focused on improving learning curves, and building collaborative trajectories.

Timely Fee Compliance

Fulfill fee schedules properly inside specified target dates. Timely compliance allows the school administration to consistently run superior assets, systems, and programs.

Official Channels Only

Direct all concerns to the School Office or use the approved parent portal. Avoid contacting academic teachers on private social circles or during regular class sessions.

Basic Protocol

Diary & Attendance Compliance

School Diary Rules for Parents

  • School diary should be checked daily for homework entries and teacher remarks without fail.
  • Parent signature required where instructed to acknowledge the notifications or comments.
  • Circulars / notices should be read carefully and preserved safely for reference dates.
  • Homework remarks and specific progress flags should be monitored and guided at home.
  • Diary must be kept safe and neat, preventing tearing, scribbling, or misplacement of sheets.
  • Important school communication or official letters must not be ignored under any condition.

Attendance & Leave Rules

  • Ensure regular attendance of your ward to keep academic progress flowing in order.
  • Students should reach school on time (strictly before 8:50 AM assembly call).
  • Leave only for genuine reasons should be taken, supported by a formal written note in the diary.
  • Inform school in case of absence immediately through parent portal or direct office line.
  • Medical leave should be communicated properly, with doctor certificate if exceeding three days.
  • Avoid unnecessary absence during academic days, tests, or general evaluation schedules.
Daily Readiness

Uniform & Academic Support

Uniform & Discipline Support

  • Send child in proper clean uniform, well-washed, neat, and precisely ironed daily.
  • Shoes must be polished and clean; check white socks and canvas on specified drill days.
  • Hair and nails neat; ensure simple styles, braided strands, and no colorful rings or jewelry.
  • Books and school materials checked daily matching their schedule timetables.
  • Encourage discipline at home by forming polite language habits and structured wake-up clocks.
  • Ensure ID card / diary carried if required, avoiding disciplinary entries in school records.

Homework & Academic Support

  • Monitor homework daily, giving guidance and explaining questions without doing it yourself.
  • Provide study support at home, ensuring a peaceful, well-lit, and distraction-free workspace.
  • Encourage reading habits, simple English journals, mathematics exercises, and general logic daily.
  • Track exam schedules and note assignments, guiding the child to distribute effort early.
  • Review report cards, identifying weak areas and setting realistic positive improvement keys.
  • Discuss progress positively, enhancing confidence rather than comparison or negative pressures.
Office & Visiting Code

Visiting & Admin Guidelines

Parent School Visiting Rules

Meet Teachers Only During Visiting Hours

Utilize designated parent interaction windows to discuss progress without disrupting lecture plans.

Prior Appointment Preferred

Log your meeting requests on the school hotline in advance to secure individual coordinator availability.

Avoid Classroom Interruption

Under no circumstances should parents walk into corridors or class sessions during study hours.

Use Office Channel for Requests

Direct all applications, document updates, and payment files solely to the central Admin Desk.

Respect Procedures & Positive Communication

Foster politeness when addressing all tutorial and non-tutorial staff to model decency for children.

Fee & Admin Responsibility

Pay Fees On Time

Clear termly or quarterly dues before scheduled deadlines to support school administration machinery.

Keep Receipts Safe

Store all physical payment tokens, bank scroll slips, or receipts for fee validation audits.

Update Contact Details Promptly

Submit active phone numbers, secondary mobiles, and home coordinates to the school ledger system without delay.

Inform School of Address / Phone Changes

Register revised residential profiles so that primary SMS warnings reach your device immediately.

Respond to Official Notices & Use Office Channel

Connect with the clerk to get transfer records, certificate signatures, or general administrative help.

Values & Safety

Pickup & Positive Partnership

Pickup & Transport Responsibility

  • Parents should arrive on time during afternoon bells to take their children home cleanly.
  • Avoid gate crowding, leaving open spaces for buses and children to disperse orderly and freely.
  • Inform the school office immediately of any sudden changes in daily transport or bus routing.
  • Ensure basic child safety and teach proper queue discipline during boarding operations.
  • Authorized pickup only; do not send strangers or unverified relatives without diary authorizations.
  • Follow gate discipline, cooperating with student prefects and campus guards.

Positive Parenting Partnership

  • Encourage discipline, punctuality, and personal integrity at home through positive modeling.
  • Build respect for teachers, helping your ward value their comments and guidelines highly.
  • Support moral values, integrity, daily courtesy, and genuine helpfulness inside child interactions.
  • Promote a healthy routine with fixed bedtime hours, nutritious balances, and restricted screens.
  • Encourage independent learning and self-management, allowing minor choices for child growth.
  • Build confidence and good behavior; praise diligent effort rather than only scores.
