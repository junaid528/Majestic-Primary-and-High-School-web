Safety & Security

A Protected Academic Circle

At Majestic Primary and High School, the safety of our student community is our structural foundation. We operate a highly controlled and vigilantly monitored campus ecosystem to insulate our students from outside disruptions while they study.

Vigilant Infrastructure

Campus Safety Overview

Secure Campus Entry

Single-gated entry strictly monitored under strict continuous gatepass controls.

CCTV Monitoring

Comprehensive coverage with active high-definition surveillance feeds.

Staff Supervision

Class tutors and floor guards regularly coordinate ground monitor patrols.

Student Safety First

Absolute mental and physical safety protocols across all campus quadrants.

Emergency Response

Immediate protocols and direct access to first-aid medical support.

Safe Exit & Pickup

Systematic exit with verification checks during afternoon dismissal.

Operations Standards

Core Protection Protocols

24/7 HD CCTV Surveillance

High-definition cameras are strategically arranged across all classrooms, academic corridors, campus hallways, playground regions, and peripheral entry boundaries.

Controlled Access Gate

Our main perimeter gates are manned by highly trained round-the-clock guards. No external visitors, parents, or staff can enter without logging biometric/visitor credentials.

Mandatory Student ID Cards

Every enrolled student and supporting staff member is provided with official smart chip ID cards. They must be worn securely at all times to identify active members.

Safe Transit Operations

For students using school-prescribed transportation systems, specialized attendants ensure children board and exit vehicles inside absolute safety perimeters.

Academy Safety Code

Student & Campus Security Rules

Student Safety Rules

  • Walk calmly and in single file in corridors, showing extreme decency and spatial discipline.
  • No pushing or running on stairs; always hold handrails and walk orderly in queues.
  • Maintain pin-drop silence and structured discipline during the morning or special assemblies.
  • Use all school properties, play equipments, and devices with proper safety care and guidelines.
  • Report any hazard, spills, raw actions, or unsafe situations immediately to the nearest class tutor.
  • Follow teacher instructions or emergency alarms with extreme speed, silence, and calmness.

Classroom & Campus Security

  • Keep the classroom space neat, tidy, and absolutely hazard-free to avoid slips/falls.
  • Absolutely no rowdy physical rough play, aggressive acts, or teasing on school premises.
  • Respect specialized safety guidelines set within science laboratories, libraries, and design labs.
  • Avoid entry to restricted areas (e.g. electrical cells, mechanical storage room, terrace gates).
  • Keep school bags, personal lockers, valuable diary log, and stationery items safely compiled.
  • Protect and conserve campus green trees, boundary flowerbeds, walls, and common infrastructure.
Transit & Access

Transport & Visitor Safety

Transport & Gate Safety

Proper Boarding Position

Wait in designated parent-monitored queues inside safe pedestrian boarding areas.

Careful Transit Motions

Enter and exit transport vehicles only after complete, silent wheels-stop.

Vehicle Safety Etiquette

Follow strict student discipline rules within school vans, never reaching out of windows.

On-time Pickups

Parents should arrive punctually for pick-ups to prevent child waiting hazards.

Keep Gate Clutter-Free

Avoid gathering or crowding around exit paths during afternoon bell dismissals.

Official Notices

Submit written letters to the coordinator immediately in case of route changes.

Visitor & Parent Safety

Gate Entry Controls

Access campus strictly via the authorized Main Gate and log official bio details.

Prior Coordinator Connect

Secure appointment confirmations prior to meeting head tutors or admins.

ID Verification Checks

Submit valid photo identity card or parent portal slips for child checkout verification.

Emergency Records Updates

Ensure parents keep updated and working cellular numbers listed with the office database.

Support Security Guards

Always cooperate patiently with security inspectors on bag/vehicle check drills.

Wellness & Resilience

Emergency Preparedness & Health

Emergency Preparedness

  • Maintain active, orderly participation during scheduled school-wide fire drill drills.
  • Memorize color-coded arrows mapping out emergency exits and corridor assembly layouts.
  • Obey direct teacher voice commands and coordinate class leaders without pushing or shouting.
  • Stay quiet, collected, and highly focused during unexpected situations to execute evacuation paths.
  • Report directly to the designated class gathering points upon evacuating classrooms.
  • Report any smoke, electrical sparks, or physical damage observed within sections immediately.

Health & Personal Safety

  • Maintain high personal hygiene, wash hands after games and washroom use.
  • Keep hydrated by drinking clean water regularly from school supplies or custom bottles.
  • Inform the class teacher immediately if you experience dizziness, exhaustion, or feel unwell.
  • Abide by playground safety instructions, avoiding dangerous objects or extreme sports.
  • Strictly forbid bringing sharp metals, pocket cutters, or matches into class spaces.
  • Build awareness of basic school first-aid locations and safety nurse clinic rooms.

Cooperative Security Checks

To keep the school completely safe, we solicit the active cooperation of parents in these safety routines:

Pickup Credentials

No student will be sent with neighbors or extended relatives unless prior written consent with proof of identity of the picker is provided to the Office.

Emergency Contact Numbers

Maintain fully active emergency phone contacts in our records. Notify the school office immediately if there is any change.

Safety Mock Drills

The school conducts periodic evacuation and fire drill practices. Students are encouraged to participate seriously to learn practical resilience.
