Chronology

General Daily Routine

Entry & Settling

Students enter the school premises, lock their belongings, and settle peacefully to get ready for prayers.

08:50 AM – 09:00 AM

Morning Assembly

An environment of calmness and respect is fostered through prayers, national anthems, pledge acts, and announcements.

09:00 AM – 09:15 AM

Academic Periods

Rigorous instructional sessions on science, math, literature, and languages occur sequentially.

09:15 AM – 10:35 AM

Recess

Students enjoy a short, necessary interval to stretch, hydrate, and relax in their homerooms.

10:35 AM – 10:45 AM

Academic Extension

Subject classes resume actively alongside customized student workshops and assignments.

10:45 AM – 12:45 PM

Lunch Break

A mid-day healthy dining break where students bond, enjoy their meals, and refresh.

12:45 PM – 01:25 PM

Labs, Sports & Arts

Practical exploration in sciences, active sports fields, creative paintings, or computer coding exercises.

01:25 PM – 03:15 PM

Final Bell & Dismissal

Reflections, notebook logging, check-marks, packing schoolbags, and disciplined, safe departures.

03:15 PM – 04:00 PM
Discipline & Integrity

Rules & Guidelines

Parent Visiting Rules

  • Parents are urged to meet class mentors or subject teachers exclusively during regular visiting hours (3:30 PM – 4:30 PM).
  • Prerecorded or prior appointment reservations are preferred for detailed feedback, avoiding unnecessary waiting.
  • No visitor is allowed to interrupt the flow of active classrooms or visit core teaching classrooms without office approval.
  • Respect for general school decorum, discipline codes, and registered entry protocols must be maintained at all times.
  • Any emergency issues or critical reports must be brought directly to the notice of the main Administrative Office.

Student Attendance & Punctuality

  • Every student must be inside the perimeter gates before the commencement of the assembly (prior to 08:50 AM).
  • Late arrivals will be logged in the index page of the student's primary school handbook for parental acknowledgment.
  • Consistent and regular attendance is key; 85% attendance across academic semesters is required for promo qualifications.
  • The school diary or handbook must be checked and signed regularly when student achievements or reports demand it.
  • Maintaining strict uniform standards, personal neatness, and punctual attendance is non-negotiable.
Protocols & Safety

Holidays & Transportation

Holiday & Weekend Notes

Sunday Holiday

Sunday remains the core weekly holiday for all academic blocks, classes, and administrative cells.

Second Saturday Holiday

The second Saturday of each month is a regular declared holiday for both students and teaching mentors.

Government Calendar list

Karnataka state public calendar holidays and emergency environmental state notices are fully applicable.

Exam & Review Adjustments

Official semesters, terminal reviews, or board tests might lead to early releases or changes in bell timings.

Transport & Student Pickup

Prompt Parental Pickups

Parents picking up children in private vehicles must arrive near the dispersal zones at least 10 minutes prior to bells.

Gate Crowding Prevention

Crowding near the core entrance gates is prohibited. Please stay inside waiting zones for child assembly lines.

Orderly Dispersal & Security

Buses, school vans, and walkers leave in well-guarded cohorts with active staff supervision to ensure maximum safety.

Routing and Address Updates

Any transport alignment change, route updates, or contact revisions must be sent in writing to the administrative office.

Important Timing Notes

  • Punctuality Constraint: All students must align their entry with 08:50 AM to settle calmly before assembly. Late entries will be documented in the school diary.
  • Second Saturday: The second Saturday of every calendar month is a declared holiday for students and academic faculty.
  • Early Departure: Leave of absence for students before the official dismissal bell require written signoffs from the Parents, approved explicitly by the Principal.
