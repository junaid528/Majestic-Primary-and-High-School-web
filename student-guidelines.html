Developing Character, Honor & Excellence

Student Guidelines

Code of Personal Excellence

At Majestic, discipline is not a constraint, but an empowering force. It builds consistent habits, shapes character, and establishes true self-respect. Every student is expected to wear their identity as a Majestician with deep responsibility, honor, and pride.

Core Pillars

Daily Student Expectations

Reach Before Assembly

Students must be in the school perimeter and settle quietly prior to the assembly bell at 8:50 AM.

Wear Proper Uniform

Wear the prescribed, neatly pressed school uniform, shoes, and tie daily to project a cohesive image.

Carry Diary & Books

Always bring the official handbook along with textbooks strictly arranged as per the daily class timetable.

Maintain Punctuality

Align all actions with the daily operational timetable, respecting transition bells and activity rosters.

Respect Everyone

Speak politely to instructors, school management, administrative staff, and your fellow student peers.

Follow Class Discipline

Adhere to active learning codes, cooperate with classroom instructions, and participate meaningfully.

Operational Directives

Institutional Policies & Code

Uniform & Grooming

The school uniform in harmony builds a neat, equal, and disciplined learning space. Cleanliness in grooming represents deep self-respect.

  • Standard Prescriptions: Clean ironed shirt, trousers/skirts, tie, belt, clean nails, socks, and highly polished black school shoes.
  • PT Uniform: Prescribed physical education athletic kit alongside clean white canvas footwear on scheduled PT days.
  • Grooming & Hair: Boys must maintain a simple, short academic haircut. Girls must tie/braid hair safely. High personal hygiene and trimmed nails are necessary.
  • Accessories Limit: No flashy wristbands, heavy decorations, cosmetics, or expensive jewelry should be worn. Always hold a valid student ID card.

Attendance & Punctuality

Consistent, uninterrupted learning is critical. Attendance is meticulously tracked to help foster great learning routines.

  • Mandatory Percentage: A cumulative attendance minimum limit of 85% is strictly enforced across classes for seasonal promotions.
  • Leave Clearances: Legitimate written leave notes compiled by parents are vital for absences. Medical absence of 3+ days demands doctor certification.
  • Consecutive Late Log: Arriving late directly results in manual warnings registered on the student diary and review reports with parents.

Classroom Behavior

A supportive, cooperative classroom atmosphere ensures deep focus, active assimilation, and mutual academic growth.

  • Silence & Focus: Stay completely quiet and attentive while academic content is presented by teaching faculty.
  • Raise Hand Style: Always raise your hand to gain permission before addressing the lecture or expressing opinions.
  • Zero Peer Abuse: Respect all classmates deeply. Bullying, physical scuffles, mockings, or harsh teasing calls are completely forbidden.
  • Pristine Classroom: Avoid causing scratches on school furniture, writing on boards without consent, and throw trash inside bins.

Exam & Academic Discipline

A student's assessment journey reflects true personal honesty and academic caliber. Compliance with exam rules is non-negotiable.

  • Absolute Academic Honesty: Avoid any copying, whispering, paper swapping, or carry unauthorized notes inside test rooms.
  • Required Stationery: Be prepared by bringing writing materials (pens, scales, geometry tools). Sharing is strictly banned.
  • Supervisor Command: Obey all special room seating rules and timing guidelines set by examination invigilators.
  • Regular Habits: Submit exam papers cleanly within times. Practice continuous regular testing and study patterns daily.
Student Handbook

Diary & Digital Responsibilities

School Diary & Homework

  • The School Diary must be carried to school every academic day without exception.
  • Daily homework tasks and learning goals must be carefully recorded in the diary.
  • Parents should check the diary daily, monitor home assignments, and sign off regularly.
  • All notices, warning lines, or circulars pinned inside must be read thoroughly by parents and students.
  • The diary must be kept absolutely clean, neat, tidy, and safely handled throughout the session.

Digital & Responsible Behavior

  • Use computers, internet ports, science labs, and visual aids strictly for academic assignments.
  • Never access forbidden websites, change configurations, or attempt unauthorized logins on school networks.
  • Misuse of hardware, lab models, digital screens, or library accessories will face instant penalty.
  • Promote helpful, healthy, and clean communication during digital labs and classes.
  • Protect and maintain the school's high-grade computing machinery, robotics gears, and digital materials with care.
Security Protocol

Transport & Campus Safety

Corridor Etiquette

Walk orderly in single file on the left side of corridors. Rowdy behaviors or blockages are barred.

Staircase Discipline

Never push, pull, or run on the stairways. Always hold handrails and descend in standard student queues.

School Gate Rules

Strictly follow security rules at gates. No students are allowed to exit the school perimeter without parent gate passes.

Safe Bus Boarding

Board the school bus or vans in calm lines. Remain seated inside, keeping hands and head within boundaries.

Emergency Reporting

Report any hazards, injuries, sickness, or damages to the nearby teacher or the administrative block immediately.

General Security Code

Respect every safety guard, security camera setup, hazard guidelines, and emergency assembly point drills.
